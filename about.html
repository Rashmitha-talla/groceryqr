About GroceryQR

Our Story

Unlike e-commerce platforms, where customers can instantly view their cart total and apply discounts or remove items accordingly, physical stores lack such an intuitive system.

Shoppers must manually track prices, which can be difficult when purchasing multiple items. A real-time budgeting tool will empower customers to monitor their expenses seamlessly, enhancing financial awareness and control.

Many customers rely on mental calculations, handwritten lists, or mobile calculators to estimate their total spending while shopping. However, these methods are often inaccurate and time-consuming, leading to miscalculations and financial strain. A system that automatically updates the total cost as items are added will streamline the shopping experience and eliminate the hassle of manual tracking.

Objectives

  • QR Code Integration .
  • Shopping Cart Management.
  • Budget Estimation .
  • Real-Time Cost Calculation .
